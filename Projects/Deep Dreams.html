Deep Dreams

Febuary 2023

Deep Dreams is an immersive pool map where you and your friends can hang out together, make smoothies, splash in the water and play with fishes.
It is a project made by Team VibeZ, a group of friends who are passionate about creating worlds and experiences.
Together we participate in the Metaverse Maker Competition every year, a competition where you get one month time to create anything you want and compete in various categories.
Deep Dreams was our entry for the Social World category, and subsequently won first place.

This project was made in NeosVR, a social VR platform which has since been shut down.
But fortunately everything lives on in Resonite, A new platform from most of the original developers.
