Recursion Lab

Febuary 2024

Recursion Lab is a social vr world built for Resonite, during MMC 2024. A competition where you have 1 month to build anything you want and compete in one of many categories.
The world features many different gadgets and toys to play with, a big interactive camera that controls the world lighting and a fully playable instrument, both for VR players as well as desktop players.

This project was made in Resonite, a social VR platform where you can work together on projects and worlds with friends. And see anything you do affect the world in real time.
At the time this won first place for the Social worlds category, and was later featured in a Resonite showcase.
