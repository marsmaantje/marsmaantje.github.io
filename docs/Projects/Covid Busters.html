Covid Busters

2022-2022

This game was made and designed as part of the Customer Project in CMGT at Saxion University.
Play as a nurse who has to herd sick and healthy NPCs to their houses and hospitals.
You should be careful because the infection spreads between the NPCs and even to you.
Eradicate the virus and save the town.

This project was made in Unity in a three week timeframe. With all assets handmade by us.
It was made by a team of 6 people, including myself.
